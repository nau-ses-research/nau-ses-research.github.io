Research Impact School of Earth and Sustainability


**Northern Arizona University**

The School of Earth and Sustainability (SES) at Northern Arizona University conducts world-class research in Earth sciences, environmental sustainability, and climate science. Our faculty and students publish in leading journals, advancing knowledge that addresses the most pressing challenges facing our planet.

Over the past 16 years, faculty from NAU's School of Earth and Sustainability have published 1,091 peer-reviewed scientific studies in the world's leading journals, with a total of 58,330 citations. Since 2009, the School has a collective H-Index of 90.
Since 2009, 195 SES Graduate students have published 369 publications in journals, including Quaternary Science Reviews, Nature Communications, Geophysical Research Letters, The Holocene, Climate of the Past. These articles have been cited 12,421 times.
In 2024, SES Faculty published 94 articles, Graduate students published 31, and Undergraduates published 0 papers in the scientific literature.
